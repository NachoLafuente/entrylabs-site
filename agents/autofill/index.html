How Autofill works.

STEP 01
Rep finishes a call. Notes land in Granola, Fathom, Gong, Otter, or Fireflies. Attio's webhook fires the moment the note attaches to a company or deal.
STEP 02
Autofill reads the note and pulls per-company RAG (your last 90 days of calls), so it extracts in your team's vocabulary, not a generic schema.
STEP 03
Claude extracts your methodology fields. MEDDIC, SPIN, BANT, or whatever you call them, captured during onboarding. Each value carries a confidence score and a citation back to the call.
STEP 04
Fields land in Attio with idempotency keys. End-to-end p95 under 30 seconds. Day-15 correction call locks the prompt to your business; monthly reviews keep it sharp.
