Flow

How it works

  1. 01

    Sales rep finishes a call.

    Notes land in Granola/Fathom/Gong/etc. Attio webhook fires when the note attaches to a company or deal record.

  2. 02

    Agent reads the note + retrieves past-call context.

    Per-company RAG pulls relevant prior calls so the agent extracts in your vocabulary, not generic.

  3. 03

    Claude extracts your methodology fields.

    MEDDIC, SPIN, BANT, custom — configured during onboarding. Each field carries a confidence score; only ≥90% writes to Attio.

  4. 04

    Fields land in Attio.

    Idempotency keys prevent duplicate writes. End-to-end p95 under 30 seconds.

  5. 05

    Day-15 correction call.

    You show me what the agent got wrong; I feed your corrections back into the prompt. The agent gets sharper every month.
