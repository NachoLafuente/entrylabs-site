STEP 01
Rep types /find Sarah at Acme in any Slack channel. Slack POSTs to the agent endpoint inside your VPC.

STEP 03
If not, Claude picks the next step: Prospeo for email, BetterContact for phone, LinkedIn lookup for role and seniority. Each call carries a confidence, and Claude stops when it has enough.
