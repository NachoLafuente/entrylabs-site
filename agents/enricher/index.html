Get started

A find is a decision, not a query.

STEP 01
Rep types /find Sarah at Acme in any Slack channel. Slack POSTs to the agent endpoint inside your VPC.
STEP 02
Enricher checks Attio first — your CRM is the cheapest, fastest, and most accurate source. If it's there, we're done.
STEP 03
If not, Claude picks the next step: Prospeo for email, BetterContact for phone, LinkedIn lookup for role and seniority. Each call carries a confidence — Claude stops when it has enough.
STEP 04
Result replies in-thread with every field, every source, every confidence. Reps decide whether to write it back to Attio with one click.
